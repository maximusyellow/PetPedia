There are multiple different animals that can be pets, the most common are hamsters, mice, birds and rabbits.

Breed: Syrian Hamster

Animal Type: Rodent

Size: 6 - 7 inches

Life Span: 2 – 3 years

Colour: Black, Cream, Gold, Brown, White, and many more.

Personality: Syrian hamsters are widely considered to be the friendliest hamster breed and the best breed of hamster for families with children. They are also the most popular of the big hamster breeds.

Breed: Chinchilla

Animal Type: Rodent

Size: 1 - 1.5 lbs

Life Span: 10 years

Colour: Black, Cream, Gold, Brown, White, and many more.

Personality: Chinchillas can make wonderful pets for the right family. They are smart, inquisitive, and loving animals. They are active, vocal, and entertaining to watch. Since they can live up to 20 years they can be long time companions

Breed: Ferret

Animal Type: Weasel

Size: 1.5 - 4.5 lbs

Life Span: 6 – 10 years

Colour: White, and Cream

Personality: Like all species, the ferret personality is unique to each individual. Some ferrets climb, jump, cuddle and give kisses but some don’t. This is their unique personality and it further develops with age and experience. Love and kindness foster the development of the best ferret personality.

Breed: Boa Constrictor

Animal Type: Snake

Size: 8 - 10 feet

Life Span: 10 - 15 years

Colour: Brown, Grey and Cream

Personality: Goes without saying, boa constrictors make a stunning pet for the right pet owner. As such, they belong to a family of snakes and are often mistaken as pythons because of similar personality traits. Boa constrictors are mainly found in the hot tropical regions of the world.

Breed: Iguana

Animal Type: Reptile

Size: 6 - 7 feet

Life Span: 20 years

Colour: Blue, Brown, Grey, BWhite, and Red

Personality:The iguana is classified as a "reptile", which tells us not much, the type of BODY i.e., cold blooded system, no hair, not yielding milk, but these things do not tell us anything about the character or the being, and that's the best part. Essentially, the iguana is quite the "same" as other earth creatures. He wants to breathe, eat his vegies, get some sun, have a safe place to sleep and live another day in the busy jungle. He clings to life and is very, very afraid of being hurt.
