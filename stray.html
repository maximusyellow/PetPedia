Stray Pet Care

If you happen to come across a stray pet, it is recommended that you contact animal control in your area in order for them to locate the missing owners. If the pet has no owner, you can adopt the pet provided that you take necessary procedures to ensure that the per is fully vaccinated and treated and any infections

Important: If you get a stray animal take it to the vet as soon as possible, this is just tips to keep them safe before you can take them to the vet.

Older Animals: The first thing to do when you rescue a stray pet is to isolate from little children and your other pets. Then clean them up, if it is an adult dog or cat, wash their body with soap and lukewarm water, then wipe their face with a wet towel. Do not give them too much food but give them some food and water. Finally give them a comfy cat/dog bed to stay in.

Younger Animals: For a young puppy or new born, wipe them down with a wet lukewarm towel. For new born puppy or kitten slowly feed them, if they are only a week old hey will not be able to eat by themselves, so they need to be feed. After they are fed, a young animal about 1 week old will have a hard time going to the bathroom, to help with that get a lukewarm towel and rub the areas they would go potty and clean it up after. Finally give them some nice place to rest in and maybe put a heating pad under a blanket to mimic they mothers warmth.
