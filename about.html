About Us

This website was made to provide information about animals and their care, it is intended to help users to decide what type of pet to get, and help users get proper care for their animal.Information about different types of animals, different breeds, their personality traits and more are provided in this website. There is also information on what to do with a stray pet, we do not give medical advice, but advice on what to do before they can take it to the vet.
