Pet Pedia

Pet Encyclopedia

Pet Pedia is a website intended to provide current/future pet owners with valuable information in order to provide the best care to the pet. The website will have information on different breeds of animals as well as dietary needs, vaccination information and shelter/animal control contact information. This is intended to be a reliable source of accurate information on pet care, and will have the contents of the site expanded by the administrators.
